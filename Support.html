Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I get started with AI implementation?

Start with our AI Implementation Checklist. It provides a step-by-step guide to implementing AI in your business, from planning to execution. The guide includes 50+ actionable steps and takes about 30 days to complete.

Which product should I choose first?

If you're new to AI, start with the AI Tools Starter Kit ($37). If you're ready to implement, get the AI Implementation Checklist ($47). For maximum value, consider our Business Automation Suite ($197) which includes multiple products.

Do I need technical skills to use these products?

No technical skills required! Our guides are designed for business owners and managers. We provide step-by-step instructions, tool recommendations, and implementation support to make AI accessible to everyone.

Implementation

How long does implementation take?

Implementation time varies by product: AI Tools Starter Kit (14 days), AI Implementation Checklist (30 days), Email Marketing Automation (30 days), Social Media Content Calendar (30 days), Business Process Automation (90 days).

What if I get stuck during implementation?

We provide comprehensive support including email support, phone consultations, and video calls. Our team is available 24/7 to help you overcome any challenges during implementation.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. If you're not completely satisfied, contact our support team for a full refund.

Technical Support

What if I need help with specific AI tools?

Our AI Tools Starter Kit includes detailed reviews and setup guides for 100+ AI tools. We also provide ongoing support for tool selection and implementation.

Do you provide training for my team?

Yes! We offer team training sessions, workshops, and ongoing support to ensure your entire team can effectively use AI tools and automation.

Can you help with custom automation workflows?

Absolutely! Our Business Process Automation guide includes custom workflow templates, and our team can help you design automation solutions for your specific business needs.
