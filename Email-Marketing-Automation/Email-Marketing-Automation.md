## Best Practices

### Email Design
- **Mobile Responsive** â†’ 60% of emails opened on mobile
- **Clear Subject Lines** â†’ Keep under 50 characters
- **Personalization** â†’ Use first names and company names
- **Single Call-to-Action** â†’ One clear action per email
- **Professional Branding** â†’ Consistent colors and fonts

### Content Strategy
- **Value-First** â†’ Provide value before selling
- **Educational Content** â†’ Teach before you sell
- **Storytelling** â†’ Use case studies and success stories
- **Urgency** â†’ Create appropriate urgency
- **Social Proof** â†’ Include testimonials and reviews

### Timing & Frequency
- **Best Send Times** â†’ Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM - 2 PM
- **Frequency** â†’ 1-2 emails per week for nurturing
- **Segmentation** â†’ Send relevant content to each segment
- **Testing** â†’ Continuously test and optimize
- **Compliance** â†’ Follow CAN-SPAM and GDPR rules
